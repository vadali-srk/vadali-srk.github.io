Research Philosophy

My research integrates mathematical sciences with engineering practice. I am interested in transforming rigorous statistical inference into deployable technologies for communication systems, intelligent sensing, signal processing and autonomous engineering. Rather than pursuing mathematical optimality alone, my work focuses on balancing optimality with practicality, leading to real-world technologies addressing strategic, industrial and societal applications.
