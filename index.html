Research Philosophy

My research focuses on bridging mathematical theory with practical engineering through statistical signal processing, intelligent sensing and autonomous systems. Rather than pursuing mathematical optimality alone, the emphasis is on developing deployable technologies capable of solving complex real-world problems in strategic, industrial and societal environments.
