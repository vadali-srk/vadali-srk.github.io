Research Vision

My research aims to bridge rigorous mathematical theory with deployable engineering systems. Rather than treating mathematical statistics and engineering as separate disciplines, I investigate how statistical inference, estimation theory and signal processing can be translated into practical technologies for communication systems, intelligent sensing and autonomous robotics.
